{% trans "Your advantages" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} wger Workout Manager is a free, open source web application that manages your exercises, workouts and nutrition. It started as a pet project of mine to replace my growing collection of different spreadsheets, but it has become something other people could also find useful. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Workouts" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} You can create and manage flexible workout routines for any goals you have. Select exactly which exercises you'll do and how many repetitions, time or distance you want to do them. You can also combine different workouts into a schedule. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Nutrition" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Create your personal diet plan by creating as many meals with as many different ingredients as you need. The application will calculate the nutritional values (total energy, protein, carbohydrates, and so on) about the whole plan as well as the indivual meals. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Diary' %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Enter the weights and repetitions you did for each exercise to generate diagrams that let you see at a glance how well you are doing. The raw numbers are of course still accessible. {% endblocktrans %} {% trans "You can also enter notes and a general impression (great, neutral, etc.)" %}

Centrailzed point of information

Sign up your gym and have trainers, managers and users join your gym from all over the world. Wger gives you an opportunity to reach boundaries beyond your reach. Manage your gym, activate and deactivate members, track users and their development as you watch your gym's potential grow.

Wger is designed by a holistic group of developers to be responsive on all devices. Access your wger information from anywhere in the world on any device accessible to you.

Wger allows you populate the database with your information. It is made for you so as to have statistical evidence and proof of record to determine your next course of action with your health.

As a trainer or gym manager, you can compare weight of two different gym users of the same gym.

Stop guessing and get realistic approximates

Wger approximates your basal metabolic rate and helps you calculate the total energy levels required to perform certain activities. Wger's weight tracker and BMI calculator help you calculate the calories you gained or lost from your nutritional plan or workout respectively. Wger provides various statistical approaches to help you track your progress on a mesocycle, microcycle and macrocycle platform.

No gain without pain. No gain without the proper diet

Wger is enriched with an abundance of weight ingredients that you can use to create a nutrition plan.

We know that your needs and uniqueness matters. Add your own ingredients, exercises, images and targeted body parts to wger. With the right diet and care from your gym trainer,you can achieve any goal. Your limitation is your own mind.
